Research Affiliation and Interests


I lead the Cybersecurity Research and Innovation Laboratory (CSRIL) at Texas A&M University - Corpus Christi. My research interests lay at the intersection of Computer Security and Software Verification and Validation. Concretely, I have experience on the development of techniques for verifying the correct implementation of access control models at the source-code level using formal specifications. In addition, I have also worked on new approaches for federated authentication and authorization based on security-related properties, a.k.a. attributes, in multi-organizational settings composed of different high-performance network providers. Recently, I have also explored approaches for enhancing the cybersecurity protection of mission-critical Energy Delivery Systems (EDS), by means of security assessment and monitoring techniques.
